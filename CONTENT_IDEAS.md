- **Saturday:** Visual content showcase

## Tips for Daily Updates

1. **Batch create content** - Write a week's worth on Sunday
2. **Keep quotes authentic** to each character's voice
3. **Vary the tone** - not every day needs to be serious
4. **Engage with current events** in the music world when relevant
5. **Use metrics** - share streaming milestones, no matter how small
6. **Tell stories** - even fictional ones add depth
7. **Ask questions** - engage your audience
8. **Cross-promote** - mention your other platforms
9. **Be consistent** - update at the same time each day if possible
10. **Have fun** - if you're excited about it, others will be too

---

**Remember:** This is YOUR project. Use these as inspiration, but let your own voice shine through!
